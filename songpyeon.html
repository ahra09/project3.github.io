Songpyeon

Ingredients

For the Rice Cake Dough:

- 2 cups of sweet rice flour (also called glutinous rice flour)

- 1/2 cup of water (approximately)

- A pinch of salt

- Food coloring (optional)

For the Filling:

- 1 cup of sweetened red bean paste

(or other desired fillings like sesame seeds or honey)

Instructions

Preparing the Rice Cake Dough:

1. In a mixing bowl, combine the sweet rice flour and a pinch of salt.

2. Gradually add water and mix until the dough comes together and has a smooth, non-sticky consistency. You may need to adjust the water amount depending on the flour's moisture content.

3. If you want to make colored songpyeon, divide the dough into several portions and add a few drops of food coloring to each portion. Knead the dough until the color is evenly distributed.

Assembling Songpyeon:

1. Take a small piece of the colored or plain dough and shape it into a small disk or ball in the palm of your hand.

2. Place a small amount (about 1 teaspoon) of the sweet red bean paste (or your desired filling) in the center of the dough.

3. Carefully fold the edges of the dough around the filling, sealing it completely. Make sure there are no holes where the filling can escape during cooking.

Cooking Songpyeon:

1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add a pinch of salt to the boiling water.

2. Gently place the songpyeon into the boiling water using a slotted spoon or strainer. Be careful not to overcrowd the pot.

3. Cook the songpyeon until they float to the surface, which usually takes 5-7 minutes. Cooking times may vary, so you can test one to ensure it's fully cooked.

4. Once they float, remove the cooked songpyeon with the slotted spoon and place them on a plate to cool.

Note: Serve your delicious songpyeon at room temperature. They are best enjoyed fresh and on the day they are made.
