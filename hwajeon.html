Hwajeon

Ingredients

- Edible flowers

- 1 cup all-purpose flour

- 1/4 cup sugar

- 1/4 teaspoon salt

- 1/2 cup water

- Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

1. Gently rinse the edible flowers and allow them to dry on a paper towel.

2. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, sugar, and salt.

3. Gradually add water and mix until you have a smooth batter.

4. Heat a non-stick skillet or frying pan over medium heat and add a small amount of vegetable oil to coat the bottom.

5. Take a small amount of batter and pour it onto the skillet to form a small pancake.

6. Place edible flowers on top of the pancake while it's still wet.

7. Cook until the edges become golden brown and crispy, then flip the pancake and cook the other side.

8. Once both sides are golden brown and crispy, remove the hwajeon and place it on a paper towel to drain any excess oil.

9. Repeat the process with the remaining batter and flowers.
