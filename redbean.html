Red Bean Porridge

Ingredients

- 1 cup of short-grain glutinous rice

- 1/2 cup of red beans (azuki beans)

- 5 cups of water

- 1/2 teaspoon salt

- Sugar (to taste)

- Optional garnish: toasted sesame seeds or pine nuts

Instructions

1. Rinse the glutinous rice and red beans separately under cold water until the water runs clear.

2. In a large pot, combine the rinsed glutinous rice, red beans, and water.

3. Bring the mixture to a boil over high heat.

4. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and simmer for about 1 to 1.5 hours, stirring occasionally, until the red beans are soft and the rice is fully cooked. You may need to add more water if the porridge becomes too thick.

5. Mash some of the red beans with the back of a spoon to thicken the porridge.

6. Season the porridge with salt and sugar to your taste. The amount of sugar can vary depending on your preference, but it’s traditionally made with a mild sweetness.

7. Serve hot and garnish with toasted sesame seeds or pine nuts if desired.
