Ssuktteok

Ingredients

- 1 cup glutinous rice flour

- 2 tablespoons mugwort (ssuk) powder

- 1/4 cup sugar

- A pinch of salt

- 1/2 cup water

- Cornstarch (for dusting)

Instructions

1. In a mixing bowl, combine the glutinous rice flour, mugwort powder, sugar, and a pinch of salt.

2. Gradually add water to the dry ingredients and mix until a smooth dough forms.

3. Divide the dough into small portions and shape them into bite-sized rice cakes.

4. Roll the rice cakes in cornstarch to prevent sticking.

5. Steam the rice cakes for about 15-20 minutes until they become translucent and slightly elastic.

6. Let them cool before serving.
