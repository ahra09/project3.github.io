Winter

[겨울]

In Winter ushers in a season of heartwarming traditional cuisine. Koreans savor various dishes tailored to combat the cold, with an emphasis on hearty, nourishing options. Fermented ingredients, prepared during the fall, remain staples, adding a burst of flavor and nutrients to winter meals. Soups and stews are cherished for their comforting warmth, while sweet and soothing dishes provide solace during the chilly months.

Activities

Kite Flying

[연날리기]

Kite flying is one of Korea's traditional folk games, and it involves flying kites made of paper or other materials. Kite flying is a popular activity not only in Korea but also in various cultures worldwide. It's an enjoyable pastime where people launch and control kites in the air.

Top-spinning

[팽이치기]

This game is often enjoyed during rural fairs, festivals, and family gatherings. Paengi-chigi involves spinning a small wooden or metal top (called a “paengi” or “top”) using one's hand to flip it over or compete with others.
