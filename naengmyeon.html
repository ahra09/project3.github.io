Naengmyeon

Ingredients

- 2 servings of naengmyeon noodles (buckwheat noodles)

- 4 cups of beef or vegetable broth

- 2 boiled eggs, sliced

- 1 cucumber, julienned

- 2 sheets of roasted seaweed, crushed

- 2 tablespoons of vinegar

- 2 tablespoons of sugar

- 2 tablespoons of soy sauce

- 1 teaspoon of minced garlic

- 1 teaspoon of sesame oil

- Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

1. Cook the naengmyeon noodles according to the package instructions. Usually, they need to be boiled for 3-4 minutes and then rinsed with cold water. Drain well.

2. In a large bowl, mix the beef or vegetable broth with the vinegar, sugar, soy sauce, minced garlic, and sesame oil to create the broth. Adjust the seasoning to your taste.

3. Place the cooked and rinsed naengmyeon noodles in a bowl.

4. Pour the cold broth over the noodles.

5. Add boiled egg slices, julienned cucumber, and crushed roasted seaweed on top of the noodles.

6. If you prefer, you can add ice cubes to make the dish even colder.

7. Serve the naengmyeon with some extra broth on the side.

8. Mix well before eating and enjoy your refreshing Korean cold noodles!
